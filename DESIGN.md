# JDB Logo Motion Design

## Style Prompt
Premium arcade-energy logo reveal with a dark warm canvas, ember glows, crisp vector edges, and a fast final lockup. The motion should feel polished and punchy without adding generic neon or changing the logo's original orange-red identity.

## Colors
- Background: #150b08
- Deep background: #070403
- Logo gold: #fec13e
- Logo orange: #f26e1b
- Logo red: #cc1700
- Shadow wine: #872b41
- Text/line warm white: #fff2d8

## Typography
- Inter, Arial, sans-serif for small technical labels only.

## Motion
- 4.8 second reveal.
- Compound-path synchronized build, warm fill snap-in, diagonal light sweep, subtle final pulse.
- Implementation uses rebuilt inline SVG paths, SVG masks, requestAnimationFrame timing, translateY motion, SVG Gaussian blur, ease-out deceleration, and CSS text fade transitions.
- Keep final frame clean with the full logo centered.

## What NOT to Do
- Do not recolor the logo away from the orange-red gradient.
- Do not use purple/blue tech gradients.
- Do not cover the logo with large text.
- Do not use a landing-page layout.
